Online Building Plan Approval System

The Online Building Permission System (OBPS) streamlines the approval process through a clear and efficient workflow. Applicants start by registering and submitting their applications online, followed by an initial verification by ULB officials. Designs are integrated with GIS and AutoCAD for thorough analysis. The application then undergoes a multi-tier review process, allowing for feedback and adjustments. Once all approvals are secured, applicants receive a digital permit, followed by post-approval monitoring to ensure compliance and support future urban planning.

Registration

The first step is to register online to create an account on the OBPS.

Application Submission

Easily submit building permission applications online, with guided forms to simplify the process.

Real-Time Application Tracking

Monitor the status of your application at any stage, providing transparency and peace of mind.

Digital Design Submission

Upload architectural designs in formats compatible with GIS and AutoCAD for seamless integration and review.

Automated Compliance Checks

Receive instant feedback on building compliance and safety regulations ensuring adherence before final submission.

User-Friendly Dashboard

Access a personalized dashboard that provides a clear overview of your applications, notifications, and deadlines.

Analytics and Reporting

Utilize analytics tools to gain insights into application trends and urban development impacts.

Support and Resources

Access a comprehensive help center with tutorials, FAQs, and customer support to assist with any inquiries or issues.
